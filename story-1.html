Jane acts as deputy for Mr F who sustained a catastrophic brain injury in a road traffic accident some years ago.  He was awarded a compensation settlement in a reported case valued in excess of £4,000,000.  Jane’s input as professional deputy for Mr F’s property and affairs was commended by the judge who heard the case in the High Court, for the careful and measured manner in which her evidence was given in respect of the costs involved in running the deputyship, and awarded that the deputyship costs would form a discrete part of the annual sum awarded to meet his needs.

Mr F’s care needs are a constant challenge and Jane and her team have worked tirelessly over the years to source the right care and maintain his therapeutic input and involvement in decisions being made on his behalf.  Being a young man, Mr F has continually wanted to express his own wishes and make as many decisions as possible for himself.  The team is committed to honouring this and ensuring that Mr F’s quality of life is enhanced wherever possible.
