Our story

Founded in 2012 JE Bennett Law is a relatively new practice, currently expanding and building on its initial success. The founder Jane Bennett has over 16 years experience as a private client lawyer, specialising in supporting vulnerable clients for the last 10 of those years. Prior to founding JE Bennett Law, Jane was a partner at two city law firms.

Jane is passionate about helping the vulnerable, and took the decision to start her own practice to have the freedom to focus on just that. Specifically, she wanted to run a firm which could concentrate on the needs of the client without the burden of commercial targets and large overheads. Today at JE Bennett , we pride ourselves on offering our clients real value and a truly personal service.

Our core business has been helping private clients on matters pertaining to court of protection. We act for those unable to help themselves and/or for their families. This role requires skills and experience in a number of legal practice areas and our work and client base has spread accordingly.
